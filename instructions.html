Picture Butterfly Instructions

Good image quality can help improve the accuracy of identification.

  1. 1. Keep butterfly centered in fram, make sure it isn’t too far away.
  2. 2. Try to avoid having cages, getting inside the cages, or other things in front of the butterfly.
  3. 3. Ensure good lighting for high contrast. If you can’t see the bird, the app can’t either.
  4. 4. Try to capture the front of the butterfly. At the very least, the head should be visible.
  5. 5. Only one species in picture. Too many butterfly in picture may result in false identification.
