Butterfly Larvae Care Management

Larval Diseases

  • Address any signs of larval disease promptly.
  • Thoroughly dissect diseased larvae to prevent viral infections.
  • Isolate healthy larvae from diseased larvae.
  • Clean the larval cabinet regularly.
  • Move diseased larvae to a separate, safe location for propagation.

Larval Care

  • Keep detailed records of larvae health.
  • Provide fresh, clean host plant leaves with sturdy stems.
  • Ensure larvae have access to adequate food.
  • Use a light stick or other appropriate tools to handle larvae gently.
  • Remove feces and waste from the larval cabinet regularly.
